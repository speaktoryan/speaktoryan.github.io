An enthusiastic & engaged consultant, researcher, and team player with a deep interest in quantitative and qualitative research methods & data analysis. Experience working with Fortune 100 companies managing many high-touch clients at once. An eye for design, a quick learner, I thrive off my desire to educate and be educated in the workplace. Always on the lookout for new opportunities to take my research, consulting, and management skills further or in a new direction. Contact me on LinkedIn above or email me at ryan@ryanwoodman.com to schedule some time to chat.


I have been interested in photography and videography since my youth, experimenting with different styles, media and content throughout the years. Since early 2018, I have been shooting for Something Blue Photography (based in Arlington Heights) doing both wedding video and photography for some of the most amazing couples I’ve had the chance to meet. My personal photography these days typically comes from my travels, of both the people I travel with and the places I’m gifted enough to be able to see. Thank you for checking out my personal work, it means a lot and I hope you enjoy the shots!
